# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, no harassment of any kind. We welcome
contributions from everyone regardless of experience level, identity, or background.

Unacceptable behavior can be reported privately to luiz.hb.esper@gmail.com. Reports are
handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and
contributors that violate this code.
